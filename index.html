Welcome to the Court!

The Kings of Tokyo (東京王) have been bringing international Drag King excellence to the Tokyo stage since 2018. Tokyo’s first and only all Drag King variety show, the Kings have been known to use weapons of mass hilarity, absurdity and titillation to bring the audience up and gender oppression down! When not holding Courts in Shinjuku 2 Chome, the Kings have been spotted raising money for queer charities in Japan and abroad.

~ Follow us, Attend a Court, Volunteer, & Bow Down to Your Majesties ~

Emmanuel Transmission

Putting the XXX back in XY

Emmanuael Transmission, otherwise known as Manny Trans or the King of Kings, has been putting the ☆performance☆ in Gender Performance since 2016. The capital's favorite legal alien, boi band Arashi-rashi member, and winner of Mr. Tokyo Closet Ball 2017, Manny's drag has been described as:

  • "A Sensual Journey into 3rd Wave Feminism's Hairy -Yet Warm, Inviting- Underbelly"
  • "The Wildest Ride You'll Get Between Shinjuku and Shinjuku 4-chome"
  • "Probably Gonna Get Him Deported One Day"

    • He founded Kings of Tokyo, the city's first and only all-King Drag Troupe, in April 2018 with the hopes of creating a home for honest weirdos of all stripes to raise up, tear down, rework, worship, tease, and otherwise lead the often camera-shy concept of the Masculine to Center Stage.

Volunteer

Interested in getting involved in our Masc-urade? The more, the merrier! We welcome EVERYONE, regardless of intersection/ability, to contribute to our Court in one of three ways:

Become a King | Become a Paige | Become a Squire

King

Do you want to strut your regal stuff on stage and show off your Masculine side? Whether Bio or original Drag King realness, comedy, dance, drama, singing, whatever, we want YOU, so apply here. No experience necessary, appreciation for Camp a plus.

Paige

It takes a lot of money to look this cheap, but it also takes a lot of help! If you are interested in helping the Kings get their makeup, props, and Emperor’s New Clothes just right, while getting free backstage pass to watch the magic happen, you might just be our next Paige. Apply here.

Squire

A more traditional “volunteer” role, the Squire position is reserved for those who want to play a role on the day of as well as help with the organization of the shows. We are always looking for Fresh Talent in Squires that can do the following:

  • PR
  • Filming/Video Editing
  • Photography
  • Costuming
  • And More!

If you think you have what it takes to be a KoT Squire, apply here!
